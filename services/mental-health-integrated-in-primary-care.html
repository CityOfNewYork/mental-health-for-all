What it is

NYC Health + Hospitals’ comprehensive care teams treat common mental health conditions in the primary care setting. Conditions that are treated include depression and anxiety. There is a particular emphasis on adolescents.

As a primary care patient, you can receive mental health screenings as part of your routine visits. If necessary, you can be referred to additional support.

Who it’s for

All New Yorkers can get mental health care at NYC Health + Hospitals.

Cost

If you have insurance, it will be billed.

If you’re ineligible for health insurance or cannot afford health insurance, you may be eligible for NYC Care.

How to get in touch

Call NYC Health + Hospitals at 844-NYC-4NYC to make an appointment.

The City has many free and low-cost substance use services.

You can also learn more from NYC Health or call NYC Well at (888) NYC-WELL for more details.
